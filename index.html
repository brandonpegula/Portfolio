Brandon Pegula

A beat down, second-hand MacBook is responsible for this letter. It’s also responsible for the hundreds (soon to be thousands) of coding hours. This old Mac has given me the distinct journey of becoming a Software Engineer.

What ignited my passion? My unstable thirst for creation. I’m a storyteller at heart so I set down my Moleskin and opened Sublime. It was time to brew install some new skillsets: Javascript, Node, React, Ruby… And tirelessly develop my ending. Front to Back.

I bring that same passion to the programming languages and scripts I’ve laid fingertips on. But I won't stop there. Sometimes you need to overclock your tenacity to successfully render your dreams. I'm an advocate of cramped hands, tired eyes, and the insatiable thirst for the next challenge.
